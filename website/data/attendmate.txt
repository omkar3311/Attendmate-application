LOGIN & ACCESS

Q: How do I log in as a student?
A:
Go to the home page.
Select "Student".
Enter your name, email, password, college ID, and classroom ID.
Click login.

Q: How do I log in as a teacher?
A:
Go to the home page.
Select "Teacher".
Enter your college ID, name, email, and password.
Click login.

Q: I forgot my login details. What should I do?
A:
Contact your teacher or college admin. They can help reset or verify your details.

---

STUDENT FEATURES

Q: Where can I see my attendance?
A:
After logging in as a student, you will be taken to your dashboard.
There you can see your attendance details.

Q: What information can I see in my dashboard?
A:
You can see:

* Total present classes
* Total absent classes
* Pending attendance
* Attendance percentage
* Date-wise attendance
* Slot-wise attendance

Q: How do I check attendance for a specific day?
A:
Go to your dashboard.
Find the date in the attendance list.
Click or view that date to see all slots for that day.

ATTENDANCE UNDERSTANDING

Q: What is attendance percentage?
A:
It is calculated based on:
Number of present classes divided by total marked classes.

Q: What is pending attendance?
A:
It means attendance is not marked yet for that slot.

Q: What is a defaulter?
A:
A student whose attendance is below the required percentage.

ATTENDANCE ACTIONS (USER GUIDE)

Q: I want to change attendance of a student
A:
Go to your dashboard.
Open the classroom.
Find and click on the student.
You will see their attendance details.
Select the date and slot.
Change the attendance (present/absent).
Save the update.

---

Q: How do I edit attendance?
A:
Open the classroom from dashboard.
Click on a student.
Go to their attendance section.
Choose the slot you want to change.
Update the status and save.

---

Q: How do I mark attendance?
A:
Go to dashboard.
Open the classroom.
Select a student.
Choose date and time slot.
Mark as present or absent.
Save changes.

---

Q: I clicked student, what next?
A:
After clicking a student, you will see full attendance details.
You can view all dates and slots.
You can update any slot from there.

---

Q: Where is attendance edit option?
A:
It is inside the student view page.
Click a student from classroom.
You will find options to update attendance there.

---

Q: Can I change past attendance?
A:
Yes.
Open the student profile.
Select the date.
Edit the attendance slot.
Save changes.

---

Q: Why I cannot edit attendance?
A:
Only teachers or admin can edit attendance.
If you are a student, you can only view it.
